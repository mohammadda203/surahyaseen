Information about Surah Yaseen

Surah Yaseen is the 36th chapter of the Qur'an and is often referred to as the heart of the Qur'an. It is a Meccan surah that is composed of 83 verses. The surah is named after the letter "Ya Sin," which is found in the first verse of the surah.
